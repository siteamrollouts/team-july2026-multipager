Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about plans, billing, and what happens when your trial ends.

What happens after my 30-day trial?

You choose a plan. All your data is preserved. If you downgrade, excess items become read-only, nothing is ever deleted.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Nothing is deleted. Items beyond your plan's limits become read-only. You can still view and export them, and you choose which items stay active.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Is there an annual discount?

Yes. The prices shown are annual billing. Monthly billing is also available at a slightly higher rate.
